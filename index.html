Insert the information in the form and the calculator will determine the total expense ratio What is the total expense ratio?
The total expense ratio is the total cost of investment to an investor who invests in an equity fund, mutual fun or an exchange traded fund.
How to calculate the total expense ratio?
total expense ratio = total fund costs / total fund Assets
Example: A new fund have been managed by binary investments. It has the following information regarding it's new fund
Total fund costs = $38,000
Total fund assets = $400,000,000
find the total expense ratio of the new fund.
To calculate TER we use the following formula
total expense ratio = total fund costs / total fund assets
total expense ratio = $38,000 / 400,000,000
total expense ratio = 9.5%
Why use the total expense ratio?
The main reason for which TER is important to investors is that it directly affects the returns on their investments.
